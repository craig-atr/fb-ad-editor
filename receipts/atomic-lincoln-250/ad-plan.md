**Business:** Atomic Tattoo Removal — laser tattoo removal, Wrentham MA (newer studio; bookings are the priority)
**Campaign:** America's 250th — "Four Score and a Fresh Start"
**Offer:** $250 off an 8-session package (seasonal, 250th tie-in)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920, ~23s total, music only (no voiceover)
**Objective:** free-consultation bookings

## Concept

Steampunk-lite Abraham Lincoln rides a hover-cycle into town to laser off a tattoo
of an old flame's name ("Ann"). A brand/story piece for America's 250th.
