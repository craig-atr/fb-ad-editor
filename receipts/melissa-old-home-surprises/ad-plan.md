## Concept

"They Don't Build Them Like They Used To… Sometimes That's the Problem." A charming old
house appears perfect until a series of humorous "surprises" reveal themselves. Melissa
Bonina helps buyers know what they're really purchasing.

### Primary text (long variant)

Original hardwood floors? Gorgeous.

Original plumbing? Maybe not.

Buying an older home can be incredibly rewarding—but knowing what you're walking into is just as important. Melissa Bonina with Custom Homes Realty helps buyers understand the hidden costs before they fall in love. Whether you're buying a charming colonial or a classic Victorian, she'll guide you every step of the way.
